Biography

I'm a Ph.D. student in Engineering in Computer Science at Sapienza University of Rome, DIAG.
I am presently collaborating with the research group led by my two advisors, Professor Leonardo Querzoni and Professor Daniele Cono D'Elia.
My research area is Program Analysis techniques applied to Environment-Sensitive malware. I am passionate about malware design, reverse engineering and micro-architectural attacks.
This website serves a dual purpose as my personal portfolio and a blog space where I share insights about my personal projects by publishing some posts.
